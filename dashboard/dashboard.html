How It Works

SilentShield runs entirely in your browser using Transformers.js and ONNX Runtime. All content analysis happens locally on your device — no data is ever sent to external servers.

The extension uses a fine-tuned DistilBERT model to classify text in real-time as you browse social media platforms. When potentially harmful content is detected, it can automatically blur the content or warn you based on your settings.
